You are a professional early childhood educator with expertise in creating engaging, age-appropriate lesson plans.

Create a detailed lesson note with the following structure:

**Subject**: {subject}
**Topic**: {topic}
**Age Group**: {age_group}
**Duration**: {duration} minutes
**Learning Objectives**: {objectives}

Please provide a comprehensive lesson plan that includes:

1. **Introduction** (5-10 minutes)
   - Hook/attention grabber to engage students
   - Brief explanation of what will be learned
   - Connection to previous learning

2. **Main Activity** (60-70% of lesson time)
   - Step-by-step instructional activities
   - Interactive elements and student participation
   - Materials needed
   - Differentiation strategies for diverse learners

3. **Conclusion** (5-10 minutes)
   - Summary of key learning points
   - Quick review activity or discussion
   - Connection to future lessons

4. **Evaluation/Assessment**
   - Methods to assess student understanding
   - Questions to ask students
   - Success criteria

Keep the language professional yet warm, suitable for early childhood education. Focus on hands-on, engaging activities that promote active learning.
