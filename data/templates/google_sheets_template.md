## How to Use This Template

1. **Create a new Google Sheet**
2. **Create two tabs**:
   - Rename the first tab to "Students"
   - Create a second tab called "Reports"
3. **Copy the column headers** from the tables above
4. **Add your own student data** to the Students tab
5. **Leave the Reports tab** with just headers - it will auto-populate

---

## Column Descriptions

### Students Tab:

- **Name**: Student's full name
- **Subject**: Primary subject or "Overall Progress"
- **Score**: Numeric score (0-100) or "N/A"
- **Notes**: Academic performance observations
- **Behavior**: Social-emotional and behavioral observations

### Reports Tab:

- **Student Name**: Name of the student
- **Report**: The generated progress report text
- **Timestamp**: When the report was generated

---

## Tips

- Keep notes concise but specific
- Use positive, growth-oriented language
- Include concrete examples when possible
- Update regularly for accurate reports
